Project Overview

The Intelligent Museum Protection System is a WRO Future Innovators project that combines automation, security, sustainability and intelligent climate control to protect artworks and reduce energy consumption.

Motto: Artists create it, we protect it.

Protection

Laser security, smoke detection and automated alarm handling.

Temperature Control

Smart decisions based on indoor and outdoor temperature and humidity.

Sustainability

Solar tracking, wind energy display, smart window control and efficient operation.

Two Controllers

Two Arduino Uno boards work together: one handles protection and climate, the other handles energy and display.

Main Functions at a Glance

The system is divided into several subsystems that work together to protect artwork, manage climate and improve energy efficiency.

Smart Protection

  • Laser security
  • Smoke detection
  • Intrusion monitoring

Smart Temperature Control

  • Indoor and outdoor temperature monitoring
  • Fireplace, air conditioning or window control
  • Automatic decision-making

Renewable Energy

  • Solar tracking servo
  • Wind and solar energy indicators
  • Energy-conscious operation

Water and Display

  • Water temperature measurement
  • Handwashing water control
  • LCD status display

System Architecture

Based on the documentation and code, the project is split into two independent control systems. This improves reliability, organization and future expandability.

Arduino #1 - Protection and Climate

  • Indoor and outdoor temperature and humidity
  • Motion, smoke and laser sensing
  • Fireplace, air conditioning, window and lighting
  • Toilet pump and vapor remover control
  • Alarm and demo mode

Arduino #2 - Energy and Display

  • LCD display and status pages
  • Solar tracking servo control
  • Solar panel and wind energy indication
  • Water temperature and hand sensing
  • Serial communication with Arduino #1
